Hamburger

A burger (short for hamburger) is a popular sandwich with a cooked ground meat patty (usually beef) in a sliced bun, topped with condiments like ketchup, mustard, and pickles, plus garnishes like lettuce, onion, and tomato, originating from Hamburg, Germany, and becoming an American staple.

Burgers come in countless variations, including cheeseburgers (with cheese) and veggie burgers, and are found everywhere from fast-food joints to high-end restaurants, with options to customize for health or taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef: 80/20 (lean/fat) is often recommended for juiciness.
  • Seasoning: Salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika.
  • Bindre/Moisture(Optional): Egg, breadcrumbs,or crushed crackers.
  • Flavor Enhancers (Optional): Worcestershire sauce, mustard, soy sauce, mincd onion.

Steps

  1. Gently mix ground beef with seasonings and optional binders (don't overmix).
  2. Form into patties, making a small indent in the center to prevent puffing.
  3. Cook on a hot grill or skillet until desired doneness.
  4. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings.
